# Return Policy

## Standard returns

You may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund,
provided the product is unused and in its original packaging. A valid receipt
or order confirmation email is required as proof of purchase.

## Refund method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of
the returned item arriving at our warehouse. Shipping costs are not
refundable unless the return is due to our error.

## After 30 days

Returns requested after 30 days of purchase are not eligible for a refund.
Between day 31 and day 90, we may offer store credit at our discretion, and
only for items that are defective or were shipped incorrectly.

## How to start a return

Contact our support team with your order number, or bring the product and
your receipt to any of our stores. We will provide a prepaid return label for
defective items.
